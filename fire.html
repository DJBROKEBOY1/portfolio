💰 Savings Rate Tracker

Your savings rate is the single biggest lever in FIRE planning — more impactful than investment returns. Most FIRE practitioners aim for 40–60%+.

🌊 Coast FIRE — When Can We Stop Investing?

Coast FIRE is the point where your portfolio is big enough to grow to your retirement goal entirely on its own. Like a snowball at the top of a hill: once it's big enough, it rolls down without you pushing.

⏳ Portfolio Longevity — How Long Does the Money Last?

Starting from your projected portfolio at retirement, this shows how long each scenario's money lasts at its withdrawal rate. Solid lines assume 6% annual growth in retirement (realistic — your portfolio keeps growing even while withdrawing). Dashed lines assume 0% growth (worst case pure drawdown). The point where a line hits $0 is when the money runs out. "∞" means the portfolio grows faster than you spend it — it never runs out!
